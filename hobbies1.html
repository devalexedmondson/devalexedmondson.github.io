Basketball

I've been playing sports all my life but have always loved to play basketball. I started playing when I was six years old and was really trying to follow in my Dad's footsteps. My Dad played college basketball at Northern Illinois University and recieved a full athletic scholorship to play there. Although I did not recieve a scholorship, I decided to play basketball in college at Benedictine University.

These days I still play basketball every chance I can. Whether thats at the park, in an open gym, or in a league, I love to get out on the court and get a good run in.
