My Journey

From Recruiter to Developer

Recruiter

I worked for a company called TEKsystems for 7 months. They are the largest IT staffing and services company in North America. In the United States they have 109 offices and in Wisconsin they have 4 offices. I sat in the Brookfield office which serviced all of the Greater Milwaukee Area. Our office worked with over 80 clients such as, Northwestern Mutual, Johnson Controls, Generac, and Rockwell.

In my time there I was able to build relationships with IT professionals in the area through various networking events and meet ups and stayed up to date on local market trends. I hit many milestones and forged great relationships while working at TEKsystems and was able to acquire insight into what companies are looking for in the Greater Milwaukee area.

The Shift

After working at TEKsystems for a while I started to realize that I was missing something in my life. I enjoyed my job due to the fact that I was able to interact with IT professionals on a daily basis. I enjoyed learning about the project that people were working on and loved hearing about the types of environments they worked in.

Even with that I still felt as if I was missing something but could never figure it out what was missing. Eventually I did. I decided to persue a career in Software Development myself by attending DevCodeCamp.

Software Developer

Right now I am attending devCodeCamp which is teaching me the skills that I need to become a Full Stack Developer. It is a three month immersive bootcamp that covers skills such as html/css, Javascript, and C#. My goal is to obtain a job in Software Development that will allow me to solve interesting business challenges every day.
